i'm an amateur woodworker, crafter of many years, embroidery artist, sticker maker, gardener, runner, and former physicist.

projects

fireplace insert

made from a disassembled box spring and secondhand wood. my own design, sun pieces cut with a scroll saw, sanded by hand, whitewashed.

snack board

made from a live edge black walnut slab. removed the bark, carved wells for snacks with a japanese spoon gouge, hand sanded and conditioned.

kitchen cart

my own design, a 6in wide wheeled kitchen cart to fit in a narrow gap between the oven and wall and with storage for sheet pans.

raised bed

a 6x3 raised garden bed built from ikea bed slats that a previous tenant left behind.

headboard

made from a disassembled box spring and attached to an existing bed frame. made exclusively with hand tools and sanded by hand.

temperature-sensing coaster

assembled a temperature-sensing circuit and attached it to a wooden coaster i made. the led lights up a different color based on whether the cup is too hot (red), too cold (blue) or just right to drink (green).

automatic cat feeder

using a cereal dispenser, servo motor, and pvc pipe, i made an automatic cat feeder. unfortunately due to mechanical limitations of the pvc pipe fittings on the output, the food got stuck and the feeder did not get used.
